Welcome to the BNPL Spending Simulation

Imagine you have €1000 in your bank account after paying all your monthly bills. You are planning for a holiday and need to make a few purchases before you leave.

Throughout this simulation, you’ll be shown a series of items. Each time, you’ll need to decide which item to buy and whether to use your debit card or a Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) option.

Keep in mind: you must manage your budget carefully so you still have money left for your holiday!
